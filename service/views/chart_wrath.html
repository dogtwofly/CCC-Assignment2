#Wrath

  • Wrath is an intense emotional state. It involves a strong uncomfortable and hostile response to a perceived provocation, hurt or threat.

  • Wrath will be categorized with the similar tricks. Each word in the tweets is lemmatized and be compared. The key words are:

  • 1. Noun: ”outrage.n.01", "madness.n.01", "rage.n.01"

  • 2. Verb: "rage.v.01", "hate.v.01"

  • 3. Adjective: "angry.a.01", "mad.a.01", "unhappy.a.01”

  • The decision boundary is 0.8 as the same as the lust classifier, and the additional help from the sentimental score is very useful to predict the wrath sins more precisely and accurately. Because the sins must intuitively be in relation to the negative emotions.
